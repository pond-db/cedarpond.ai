Space Ball

Space Ball is a fast, chaotic gravity-flip arcade game built as a single self-contained browser file. Flip between floor and ceiling, hop onto one-way platforms, dodge lasers, gates, blasters, lava pits, and survive long enough to stack score and diamonds.

The game keeps all the playful weirdness from the prototype while giving it a clean home on the Cedar Pond site. It runs right in the browser with keyboard and touch controls.

Controls

  • Space flips gravity after landing.
  • W hops opposite your current gravity.
  • Esc opens the menu and difficulty picker.
  • Left tap flips and right tap hops on touch devices.

Modes

  • Space + X for middle mode, then Z to cancel.
  • T + Y for ship mode, then G to stop.
  • Q for arrow mode, hold A to rise, E to stop.

Difficulty Rewards

  • Easy: 10,000 score = 1 diamond.
  • Middle: 1,000 score = 1 diamond.
  • Hard: 100 score = 1 diamond.
  • Difficlt: 50 score = 10 diamonds.
